What it feels like to be in Paris

Paris offers endless attractions and experiences, no matter how much time you spend here, it will never feel like enough:

  • Climb the Eiffel Tower, stroll along the Champs Elysées, explore the Louvre, attend shows and exhibitions, or simply wander by the Seine... Read in French at the Tuileries Garden, and take the time to soak in the Parisian lifestyle.
  • Embracing this whirlwind of excitement and romance will leave you with lasting memories. You'll also improve your French and realize your growing ability to communicate in the language of Molière.
